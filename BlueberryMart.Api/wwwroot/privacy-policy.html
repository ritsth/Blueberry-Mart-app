Privacy Policy

Blueberry Mart — Last updated June 2026

This policy describes what information Blueberry Mart ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the app you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Email address — required to create and access your account.
  • Phone number — optional; used to link in-store purchases and loyalty points.
  • Delivery addresses — saved addresses you add for order delivery.
  • Order history — items, quantities, prices, and status of your past orders.
  • Review photos — images you voluntarily attach to product reviews.
  • Account identifiers — an internal user ID used to associate your data.
  • Google account info — if you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account email and a Google-issued identifier. We do not receive your Google password.

2. How we use your information

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Process and deliver your orders.
  • Track and award loyalty points.
  • Display your order history and saved addresses within the app.
  • Show your product reviews to other shoppers.
  • Send optional back-in-stock and order-status notifications (only if you enable them).

3. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Your data is processed solely on our own infrastructure (Google Cloud, hosted in the United States).

We use the following service providers who may process your data on our behalf:

  • Google Cloud / Cloud Run — hosts our API and database.
  • Google Cloud Storage — stores review photos.
  • eSewa — payment processing (we share only what is required to complete a payment).

4. Data retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your email, phone number, password, saved addresses, and notification preferences are permanently removed. Order history is retained in fully anonymised form (no personal identifiers) for business records.

5. Your rights

  • Access & correction — you can view and update your profile inside the app.
  • Deletion — you can delete your account at any time from the Account tab, or by emailing us. See our account deletion page.

6. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS). Passwords are stored as salted PBKDF2 hashes — we never store your password in plain text.

7. Children's privacy

Blueberry Mart is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
