🫐 Blueberry Mart — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Blueberry Mart ("we", "us", the "app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. By using the app you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information — your email address and password (stored securely, hashed), and an optional phone number.
  • Delivery details — delivery addresses you save and the contact details needed to fulfil an order.
  • Order & activity data — items you browse, add to cart, and purchase; order history; product reviews and ratings you submit.
  • Payment information — payments are processed by our payment provider, eSewa. We receive confirmation of a transaction but do not store your full payment-card or wallet credentials.
  • Device & usage data — basic technical information needed to operate the app reliably (for example, app interactions and error diagnostics).

2. How we use your information

  • To create and manage your account and keep you signed in.
  • To process, deliver, and track your orders.
  • To operate loyalty points, membership ("Blueberry Plus"), and in-store purchase linking.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your requests, including via the in-app assistant.
  • To send you order updates, alerts, and (where permitted) offers.
  • To improve the app, prevent fraud, and keep the service secure.

3. How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only as needed to run the service:

  • With our payment provider (eSewa) to process payments.
  • With delivery personnel to fulfil your orders (e.g., your delivery address).
  • With service providers that host our backend and infrastructure, under appropriate safeguards.
  • Where required by law or to protect our rights and users' safety.

4. Data security

Your data is transmitted to our servers over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), and passwords are stored in hashed form. While no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

5. Data retention & your choices

  • We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service and meet legal obligations.
  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your account and personal data by contacting us at the email below.

6. Children's privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
