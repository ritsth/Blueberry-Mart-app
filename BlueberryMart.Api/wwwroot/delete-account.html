Blueberry Mart — Account & data deletion

Option 1 — Delete directly in the app

  1. Open the Blueberry Mart app and sign in.
  2. Go to Account (bottom tab).
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion. Your account is removed immediately.

Option 2 — Email us

If you no longer have the app installed, email us and we'll delete your account and data within 30 days:
